Smart metering

Domestic smart meter

Learn more about domestic smart meters here.

Smart meters are the next generation of electricity meters, A smart meter is a digital recorder that can provide a two way communications between the utility company and the consumer. This on-line real-time monitoring enables the utility company to match supply with demand, it can also gives the consumer information on current and past usage and charges through an in-home display unit..

So far, Electric Ireland Networks has installed 10,000 meters throughout the country and provided over 1000 in-home displays as part of their smart metering trial. This trial will determine the best technology option for Electric Ireland. The in-home displays will show the consumer real-time power consumption and the current charge rates. Retro-fitting smart meters to the existing customer base would be an expensive exercise so it is unlikely to be a feature in Irish homes for the foreseeable future.

Meter Plug

There is another option for smart metering provided by the meter plug. The meter plug is a small device that connects between your appliance and the power outlet. It is a smart plug that measures and records the power consumption of any device in the home from phone charges to air conditioning units.

The meter plug app provides real time information on usage and charges. The meter plug can be controlled remotely through the app or activated by proximity sensing. See how much each home appliance is costing will give you full control over your energy consumption and help you save hundreds of euro annually.
